# GLP Access Prior Authorization Policy

This policy describes coverage eligibility for GLP Access injectable therapy for adult patients with type 2 diabetes.

Eligibility criteria:

- Diagnosis of type 2 diabetes confirmed in the medical record.
- Recent A1C value of 7.0 percent or greater.
- Trial and failure, intolerance, or contraindication to metformin.
- Prescriber submits chart notes and current medication list.

Formulary alternatives:

- Preferred GLP-1 alternatives include GlucoLite Weekly and MetaboSure.
- If the preferred option is unavailable due to shortage, the plan may allow temporary approval for GLP Access.

Copay and access:

- Commercial plans may support copay assistance.
- Prior authorization renewals require evidence of adherence and clinical benefit.
