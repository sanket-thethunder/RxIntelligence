# Specialty Copay Assistance Policy

Manufacturer assistance programs help eligible commercially insured patients reduce out-of-pocket cost for specialty therapies.

Program requirements:

- Patient must have active commercial insurance.
- Patient must be a resident of the United States.
- Therapy must be prescribed for an FDA-approved indication.
- Program cannot be used by patients enrolled in Medicare, Medicaid, TRICARE, or other government-funded plans.

Operational workflow:

- Verify benefit coverage.
- Confirm prior authorization status.
- Offer formulary alternatives when the prescribed therapy is non-preferred.
- Route eligible patients to copay enrollment and pharmacy coordination.
