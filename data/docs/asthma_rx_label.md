# StepOne Inhaler Drug Label

StepOne Inhaler is a maintenance therapy for moderate persistent asthma. It is not intended for acute bronchospasm.

Coverage notes:

- Preferred formulary alternatives include BreathEase HFA and CortiAir Daily.
- Typical prior authorization criteria include documented asthma diagnosis, trial of one preferred inhaled corticosteroid, and prescriber attestation that alternatives were ineffective or contraindicated.
- Common safety review items include recent hospitalization, active infection, and duplicate inhaled corticosteroid therapy.

Patient support:

- Commercially insured patients may qualify for a manufacturer copay card.
- Medicare, Medicaid, and other government insurance patients are not eligible for manufacturer copay cards.
- Specialty pharmacy coordination is recommended when the plan requires restricted distribution.
